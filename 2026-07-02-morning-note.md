### Outlook: **BEARISH (near-term)**

POET's operational story — optical interposer, AI data center ramp, 1M units/month target — has genuine technological merit, but the stock is uninvestable for institutional capital until at minimum: (1) the class action trajectory clarifies (dismissal or early settlement), (2) a replacement customer for the Marvell/Celestial AI volume is publicly announced, and (3) the dilution overhang from the $400M raise and warrants is absorbed.
