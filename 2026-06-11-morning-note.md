### Key Risk Status

| Risk | Status |
|---|---|

Marvell/Celestial AI order cancellation & NDA lawsuit** | **ACTIVE / UNRESOLVED.** On April 23, 2026, Marvell Semiconductor cancelled all POET purchase orders citing breach of confidentiality after a POET executive disclosed Celestial AI shipment/invoice details in a public video interview on April 21. | **3. $400M capital raise / dilution** | **CLOSED May 18, 2026 — DILUTION ONGOING.** POET raised $400M in a registered direct offering at $21.00/share (19.05M new shares) to MMCAP International, plus a 3-year warrant for an additional 19.05M shares at $26.25. Stock dropped 22% on closing. Fully diluted share count could roughly double vs. pre-raise if warrants are exercised. Current price (~$10.25) is well below both the offering price and the warrant strike, limiting near-term warrant exercise risk but illustrating how far the stock has fallen. |

With shares down ~75%+ from pre-crisis levels, three active legal overhangs, its primary revenue relationship severed, and $400M in dilutive capital raised at prices far above current trading, POET faces an extremely challenging path to recovery absent a credible new commercial partnership or favorable legal resolution before the June 29 deadline.
