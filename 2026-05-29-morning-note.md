### Outlook: **BEARISH near-term / Speculative**

The Marvell cancellation removes POET's most visible near-term revenue catalyst, the securities class actions add legal costs and management distraction, and the $400M raise — while providing runway — signals the company is burning cash at scale. Until a new anchor customer is named and the class action deadline passes (June 29), the risk/reward skews negative. The Lumilens supply agreement and 2,495% Q1 revenue growth are real, but profitability remains distant. Position sizing discipline is critical; this is a high-volatility name with binary catalysts.
