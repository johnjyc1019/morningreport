## POET Technologies (POET) Daily Update

**Current Price:** ~$8.03 (last close reference April 28, 2026) — *live quote unavailable; check NASDAQ:POET at open*
**YTD Context:** Stock is down approximately 47–49% from pre-announcement levels following the Celestial AI order cancellation disclosed April 27, 2026.

### News in the Last 24 Hours
- **Levi & Korsinsky Shareholder Alert (June 2, 2026):** Levi & Korsinsky LLP issued a formal shareholder alert reminding POET investors of the pending securities class action, with the **lead-plaintiff deadline of June 29, 2026**. This follows similar alerts from Rosen Law Firm and Block & Leviton. No new SEC filings (Form 6-K or 20-F) were identified in the past 24 hours beyond the ongoing litigation notices.
- **June Tightrope:** Analysis in market press highlights the collision of the June 26 AGM (shareholder vote on the $400M raise) and the June 29 class action deadline as a binary-risk window.

### Key Risk Status

| Risk | Status |
|---|---|
| **(1) Marvell / Celestial AI Order Cancellation & NDA Lawsuit** | **Active / Unresolved.** On April 23, 2026, Marvell (which acquired Celestial AI) cancelled all outstanding purchase orders, citing POET management's public disclosure of the customer relationship and shipment timing on a Stocktwits YouTube interview on April 21. POET allegedly mischaracterized its NDA obligations on camera. No settlement or legal resolution reported. |
| **(2) Securities Class Actions (PFIC / misrepresentation)** | **Active — deadline approaching.** Multiple law firms (Rosen, Block & Leviton, Levi & Korsinsky) allege POET made materially false statements about its business relationships and PFIC tax status. Class period: April 1–27, 2026. Lead-plaintiff deadline: **June 29, 2026.** |
| **(3) $400M Capital Raise — Dilution Impact** | **Closed May 18, 2026.** A single institutional investor acquired ~19M new shares at $21/share plus warrants struck at $26.15, raising $400M gross. Given POET's trailing revenue of ~$1.1M (TTM), dilution is extreme. The $21 placement price acts as a psychological ceiling/resistance. Existing shareholders are materially diluted. |

### Outlook: **BEARISH / AVOID**
POET faces a convergence of unresolved legal risk (NDA litigation, class actions), massive structural dilution from the May raise, and the loss of its flagship customer relationship with Celestial AI. The June 26–29 window is a dual-catalyst minefield. No near-term revenue catalyst is visible given prototype targets remain late 2026 at earliest. Risk/reward is unattractive.
