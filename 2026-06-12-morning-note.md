## Macro Backdrop

US equity futures are little changed this morning after a strong Thursday session (S&P 500 +1.75%, Nasdaq +2.54%, Dow +1.86%), with sentiment dominated by two macro drivers: (1) **US-Iran diplomacy** — President Trump cancelled planned strikes late Thursday and signalled a deal could be signed as early as this weekend in Europe, dragging crude oil lower and lifting risk assets broadly; and (2) **SpaceX's historic IPO**, priced at $135/share to raise ~$75B, set to begin trading today as the largest IPO on record.

The 10-year Treasury is steady at **4.52%**. The Fed holds rates at 3.50–3.75%; markets are fully pricing one more 25bp hike by December. Trump's push for a **15–20% minimum tariff on all EU goods** is a new headwind, weighing on EUR/USD.

4. **Watch SpaceX IPO open** — Priced at $135; hype is extreme. Avoid chasing at open; look for a 20–30% post-IPO pullback as a more disciplined entry.

## POET Technologies (POET) Daily Update

**Price:** ~$11.56 (Jun 11 close) | **52-wk range context:** Up ~225% YoY despite recent collapse

### Recent News (Last 24h)
No material new filings or press releases identified in the last 24 hours. The stock continues to trade in the $10–12 range as the market digests the cumulative impact of the April 27 disaster and subsequent financing overhang.

### Key Risk Status

| Risk | Status |
|---|---|
| **Marvell/Celestial AI cancellation & NDA lawsuit** | Active. Marvell (which acquired Celestial AI) cancelled all purchase orders on Apr 23, 2026, citing POET CFO's video disclosure of confidential shipping/order details as an NDA breach. Stock fell 47.3% on disclosure (Apr 27). Civil and securities claims now pending. |
| **Securities class actions (PFIC tax misrepresentation)** | Active. Multiple law firms (Rosen, BGG, SueWallSt) allege POET failed to adequately disclose its Passive Foreign Investment Company (PFIC) status, creating adverse U.S. tax implications for shareholders. Lead plaintiff deadline: **June 29, 2026**. Class period: Apr 1–Apr 27, 2026. |
| **$400M raise dilution** | Closed. May 18, 2026: 19.05M shares issued + 19.05M warrants (exercise $26.25, 3-yr term) at $21.00/unit. At current price ~$11.56, stock trades at a 45% discount to the offering price — substantial dilution already reflected, but warrant overhang (~27% of pre-raise float) persists. |

### Outlook: **Bearish near-term / Speculative**
The Marvell order loss removes POET's primary near-term revenue catalyst; combined with active securities litigation (lead plaintiff deadline imminent June 29), warrant dilution overhang, and a stock trading well below the May offering price, risk/reward skews negative until the legal calendar clears and a credible revenue replacement is announced.
