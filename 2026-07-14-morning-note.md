## Macro Backdrop

Today is a rare double-print day: the **June CPI report drops at 8:30 AM ET** alongside Q2 earnings from the five largest US banks — a confluence that sets up outsized volatility in both rates and equities.

- **CPI (June, consensus):** Headline YoY expected to ease to **~3.9%** (from 4.2% in May), driven by a ~10% drop in gasoline prices as Strait of Hormuz tensions briefly eased. Core CPI expected ~2.9% YoY / +0.2% MoM.
- **Context:** May CPI was 4.2% YoY, the highest since April 2023. Inflation has remained above the Fed's 2% target for more than five years.
- **Fed:** Funds rate held at **3.50–3.75%**. New Chair Kevin Warsh dropped forward guidance in favour of pure data dependence. Markets price ~**64% probability** of a rate hike at the September FOMC meeting.
- **Rates:** 10-year Treasury yield at **4.56–4.58%**; 2-year at **4.21%**.
- **Oil:** Trump's reinstatement of a total blockade on Iranian shipping through the **Strait of Hormuz** sent Brent crude surging toward **$80/bbl** overnight (+4% intraday at peak). WTI recovering after a prior-week slide to ~$68.55; now tracking ~$71–75/bbl.

**Current Price:** ~$11–12 range (52-week high: $20.81; down ~40–60% from highs depending on entry). Citadel disclosed a 5.1–5.9% passive stake on July 8, lifting the stock ~3% on that session. No new price print confirmed for July 14 pre-market.

**News (Last 24 hours):** No new SEC filings or press releases identified overnight for July 14. Most recent catalyst was the Citadel stake disclosure (July 8). Company continues to tout 10+ active customer engagements and a planned ramp to 1M optical engine units/month by end of 2027, against only ~$1.1M in current revenue.

### Key Risk Status

| Risk | Status |
|---|---|
| **(1) Marvell/Celestial AI order cancellation & NDA lawsuit** | **Active / Unresolved.** Celestial AI (Marvell unit) canceled ALL purchase orders on April 23 after board member Thomas Mika allegedly disclosed confidential order volumes and delivery dates in a public interview. Caused a 47% one-day crash. NDA breach litigation ongoing; no settlement disclosed. |
| **(2) Securities class actions (PFIC tax misrepresentation)** | **Active / Escalating.** Three separate class-action lawsuits filed on behalf of investors who purchased POET shares April 1–27, 2026, alleging material misrepresentation of PFIC tax status and failure to disclose risks from the NDA breach. No resolution timeline visible. |
| **(3) $400M raise dilution impact** | **Severe / Ongoing.** May 2026 raise issued ~19M shares at $21/share plus warrants at $26.25 — stock currently trades ~40–60% below the offer price. Additional $150M registered direct offering also completed. Total dilution weighs heavily; management credibility damaged. |

### Outlook: **BEARISH (speculative)**

Three concurrent headwinds — a major customer cancellation with no public replacement of equivalent size, three active class-action lawsuits, and severe dilution from a $400M+ raise at prices far above market — create a structurally challenged setup. Near-term institutional interest (Citadel stake) provides a modest floor, but with only ~$1.1M in revenue and no confirmed replacement for the Celestial AI volume, the bull thesis rests entirely on execution of an unproven production ramp. Watch for any announced customer wins or legal settlements as potential re-rating catalysts.
